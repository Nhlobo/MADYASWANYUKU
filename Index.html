Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for a pest control service?

Ensure all areas are accessible, remove food items, and cover any surfaces where treatments will be applied.

Are your products safe for children and pets?

Yes, we use eco-friendly solutions that are safe for your family and pets.

How long does the service take?

The duration depends on the size of your property and the severity of the infestation. On average, services take 1-2 hours.

Do you offer a warranty for your services?

Yes, most of our services come with a 30-day warranty. We also offer extended warranties for premium plans.
